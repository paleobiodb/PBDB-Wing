Please consult the data entry tutorial and tip sheets for entering collection data, occurrences, lithologies, paleoenvironments/tectonic settings, taxonomic data, ecology/taphonomy, paleobotany data, and batch uploads. If you have further questions you can write to any member of the Executive Committee or the database administrator. Remember: enter all the data, don't enter lumped or mixed collections, and search carefully to avoid entering duplicates.
