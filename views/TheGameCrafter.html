Looking for New Tabletop Games?

The Game Crafter sells over 4000 indie board games and card games. Browse by category or search by keyword, genre, theme, and more. Customer reviews, photos, downloadable rule sheets, and videos make shopping fun and easy.

Unique Games From Around the World

Our shop is an open marketplace for indie tabletop games. Products are created by game designers around the world that use our print on demand game publishing service to produce their games.

Board Game Pieces and Parts

The Game Crafter has 900 game pieces and parts in stock! We have an excellent selection of dice, pawns, poker chips, wood bits, miniatures, meeples, card stands, tokens, spinners, gems, sand timers, and even vintage game parts!

Bulk Discounts and No Minimum Order

There is no minimum order size when you shop at The Game Crafter. Buy 1 game piece or as many as you need. Large part orders receive discounts based on quantity and the savings is automatically applied during checkout.

The Game Crafter offers game designers an easy-to-use system to make a board game, card game, or custom playing cards. Free templates, instructions, videos, and proofing tools are available to help people create quality products.

It's Easy to Make Your Own Game:

  1. Upload your artwork and rules
  2. Choose the parts in your game
  3. Select the game box you want
  4. Buy 1 copy or as many as you need

Once a game is created, the designer can choose to self-publish it in our online shop and sell it to a worldwide audience. Learn more
